Interview with
Sahak Ter-Gukasyan
Network Engineer @ Boingo Wireless

  • What interested you in the field?
  • What did you study?
  • Describe your role and your typical day at work
  • How will AI impact the field?
  • What recommendations do you have for someone entering the field?
  • Any other info?

What interested you in the field?

Sahak's passion for troubleshooting and problem-solving led him towards networking. Encouragement from and friends, who recognized his fit for the field, further fueled his interest. His fascination with technology and the opportunity to delve into the intricacies of networking solidified his decision.

What did you study?

Sahak pursued his education by getting CCNA and CCNP certifications at Brand College. He engaged in self-study and attended a three-month boot camp, which provided hands-on training and a comprehensive understanding of the field. Sahak stresses the importance of ongoing learning and staying updated with new technologies. He recommends exploring various products from many vendors (e.g. Cisco, Juniper, Ruckus) to maintain a vendor-neutral approach.

Describe your role and your typical day at work

In his role, Sahak handles diverse responsibilities on a daily basis. His routine begins with email management to stay informed about ongoing developments. He oversees projects involving network expansion for new clients and private services. Sahak tests new products in the lab and migrates them to the production environment. Troubleshooting tickets, whether personally or by coordinating with the field team, is a significant part of his work. Additionally, Sahak mentors junior engineers, creates documentation, and facilitates knowledge sharing through various mediums.

How will AI impact the field?

Sahak acknowledges the positive impact of AI on the industry, particularly mentioning tools like ChatGPT and OpenAI. He believes AI is a valuable aid in troubleshooting and obtaining quick information. Instead of manually searching through extensive documentation, Sahak utilizes AI to access relevant insights and workarounds. While concerns exist regarding job displacement, Sahak emphasizes the need to adapt and harness AI's capabilities to increase productivity and efficiency. He actively incorporates AI into his daily routine. When troubleshooting issues and analyzing log messages, he leverages ChatGPT. By copying the log message and inputting it into the AI tool, Sahak receives prompt responses that help him identify problems and potential solutions. While he acknowledges the AI suggestions may not always be entirely accurate, they serve as valuable guidance and save time by directing him in the right direction.

What recommendations do you have for someone entering the field?

Sahak offers valuable recommendations for those aspiring to enter the networking field. He stresses the importance of genuine passion and dedication. Sahak advises aspiring professionals to extensively study available resources, including YouTube, Google, and platforms like CBT Nuggets for comprehensive knowledge. He also emphasizes the value of internships for real-world exposure and firsthand experience in the networking field.

Any other info?

Sahak emphasizes the importance of genuine passion and continuous learning in the networking field. Staying up to date with current technologies is crucial for success in both networking and the broader tech industry. Sahak highlights the need for lifelong learning, dedicating time each day to expand knowledge through reading books, watching videos, and utilizing online tools for hands-on practice. He stresses that being passionate about the field makes the learning process enjoyable and not feel like work. By embracing a love for technology and being open to learning, individuals can thrive in the ever-evolving networking industry.
